MMH ASSOCIATE

A unified B2B marketplace connecting buyers, sellers, and manufacturers in one seamless ecosystem, enabling efficient sourcing, transparent trade, and scalable business growth.

Project Overview

MMH Associate is a B2B ecommerce platform designed to digitize traditional offline trading and convert it into a centralized online marketplace, similar to IndiaMART. The platform enables buyers to discover products, sellers to manage listings and orders, and manufacturers to supply products efficiently, all within a single ecosystem.

The project includes a buyer mobile app, seller & manufacturer web portals, and a comprehensive admin panel to manage the entire platform.
